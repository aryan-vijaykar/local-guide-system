# Context Management Guidelines

## Product.md Structure Requirements

### Required Sections
1. **City Information** - Basic city details and local names
2. **Common Slang & Phrases** - Local vocabulary with meanings
3. **Local Food & Street Vendors** - Timing, areas, hygiene tips
4. **Transport Habits** - Peak hours, modes, etiquette
5. **Daily Routines & Timing Patterns** - Local schedules
6. **Cultural Do's and Don'ts** - Social norms and customs
7. **Weather Patterns** - Seasonal considerations
8. **Festivals & Events** - Impact on daily life
9. **Local Pricing Expectations** - Cost ranges by category
10. **Safety Notes** - Area-specific precautions
11. **Local Logic Patterns** - City-specific rules

### Context Categories
- `slang`: Local vocabulary and expressions
- `food`: Restaurants, street food, timing
- `transport`: Trains, buses, autos, traffic
- `culture`: Customs, etiquette, traditions
- `weather`: Seasonal patterns and impacts
- `festivals`: Events and their effects
- `pricing`: Local cost expectations
- `safety`: Area and time-specific advice
- `timing`: Daily patterns and schedules

### Data Quality Standards
- All information must be location-specific
- Include timing context for recommendations
- Provide cultural reasoning for customs
- Specify confidence levels for uncertain data
- Cross-reference related information
