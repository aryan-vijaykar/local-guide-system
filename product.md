# Mumbai Local Context File

## City Information
**City Name:** Mumbai (Bombay)
**Local Name:** Aamchi Mumbai

## Common Slang & Phrases
- **Bhai** - Brother (used for everyone)
- **Scene** - Plan/situation
- **Tapri** - Small tea stall
- **Cutting** - Half cup of tea
- **Vada Pav** - Local burger (potato fritter in bread)
- **Bhel** - Puffed rice snack
- **Auto** - Auto-rickshaw
- **Local** - Local train
- **Dabba** - Lunch box
- **Gully** - Lane/street
- **Chalta hai** - It's okay/acceptable
- **Jugaad** - Creative solution/hack
- **Timepass** - Casual hanging out
- **Bindaas** - Carefree/cool

## Local Food & Street Vendors
### Street Food Timings
- **Morning (6-10 AM):** Poha, Upma, Misal Pav
- **Afternoon (12-3 PM):** Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Dabba food
- **Evening (4-8 PM):** Bhel Puri, Sev Puri, Pani Puri, Cutting chai
- **Night (8-11 PM):** Kebabs, Rolls, Ice cream

### Popular Street Food Areas
- **Juhu Beach:** Bhel Puri, Pav Bhaji (evening crowds)
- **Mohammed Ali Road:** Kebabs, Biryani (post 8 PM)
- **Linking Road:** Pani Puri, Cutting chai (4-7 PM peak)
- **CST Station:** Vada Pav stalls (morning rush)

### Hygiene Expectations
- Look for crowded stalls (high turnover = fresh food)
- Avoid places with stagnant water
- Peak hours = fresher food

## Transport Habits
### Local Trains
- **Peak Hours:** 8-11 AM, 6-9 PM (extremely crowded)
- **Off-Peak:** 11 AM-4 PM, post 9 PM (manageable)
- **Ladies Compartment:** First 4 coaches
- **General Rule:** Stand on left, let people exit first

### Auto-Rickshaws
- **Meter Rule:** Insist on meter, especially during day
- **Night Charges:** 1.5x meter rate post 12 AM
- **Sharing:** Common during peak hours on fixed routes
- **Negotiation:** Expected for long distances

### Buses (BEST)
- **AC Buses:** More expensive but comfortable
- **Regular Buses:** Crowded but cheap
- **Peak Timing:** Avoid 8-10 AM, 6-8 PM

## Daily Routines & Timing Patterns
### Office Hours
- **Start:** 9-10 AM (most offices)
- **Lunch:** 1-2 PM (dabba culture strong)
- **End:** 6-7 PM

### Shopping
- **Markets Open:** 10 AM
- **Peak Shopping:** 4-8 PM
- **Sunday:** Most markets closed

### Traffic Patterns
- **Morning Rush:** 7:30-10:30 AM
- **Evening Rush:** 6-9 PM
- **Weekends:** Lighter traffic, but malls crowded
- **Monsoon:** Add 30-50% extra travel time

## Cultural Do's and Don'ts
### Do's
- Remove shoes before entering homes
- Use "Namaste" for greetings
- Respect queue culture in trains
- Bargain at street markets (not in malls)
- Carry cash (many places don't accept cards)

### Don'ts
- Don't eat beef openly (sensitive topic)
- Don't wear revealing clothes in religious areas
- Don't refuse offered tea/food (considered rude)
- Don't honk unnecessarily (though everyone does)

## Weather Patterns
### Monsoon (June-September)
- Heavy rains, flooding common
- Trains delayed frequently
- Street food quality may vary
- Carry umbrella always

### Winter (October-February)
- Pleasant weather
- Best time for street food
- Outdoor events common

### Summer (March-May)
- Hot and humid
- Prefer AC transport
- Drink lots of water
- Avoid afternoon outdoor activities

## Festivals & Events
### Major Festivals
- **Ganesh Chaturthi (August/September):** Traffic chaos, processions everywhere
- **Navratri (September/October):** Late night celebrations, dandiya events
- **Diwali (October/November):** Markets crowded, firecrackers, sweets

### Festival Impact
- **Traffic:** Expect 2x normal travel time
- **Food:** Special festival foods available
- **Safety:** Crowded areas, pickpocketing risk increases
- **Transport:** Local trains more crowded

## Local Pricing Expectations
### Street Food
- Vada Pav: ₹15-25
- Cutting Chai: ₹8-12
- Bhel Puri: ₹30-50
- Pav Bhaji: ₹60-100

### Transport
- Auto (per km): ₹12-15
- Local Train: ₹5-15 (depending on distance)
- Bus: ₹8-25

### Safety Notes
- **Crowded Areas:** Beware of pickpockets
- **Late Night:** Stick to main roads
- **Monsoon:** Avoid low-lying areas during heavy rain
- **Festival Times:** Extra caution in crowds

## Local Logic Patterns
- "After 7 PM roads are jammed" - Plan accordingly
- "If it's raining, add 1 hour to travel time"
- "Crowded food stall = good food"
- "Never refuse chai when offered"
- "Local train is fastest, but most crowded during peak"
